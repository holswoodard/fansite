Here are some of his greatest clips and accomplishments of all time

Jerry Sienfeld sat down with his friend Larry David in 1989 to discuss plans for a sitcom. The two masterminds wanted to create a show about Jerrys life and the whole lot of "nothing" he does on a daily basis.


This idea was even poked fun at in the show. During season four the characters, Jerry and George Costanza pitch thier show idea about "nothing" to NBC executives. Elaine Benes and Kramer, Jerrys neighbor and ex girlfriend were also included as characters in the pilot.


Some of my favorite things to watch beside the show Sienfeld is the bloopers on set. The compliation of some of the most funny actors and characters laughing at each others jokes make me so happy!
